Synopsis

My Brothers and Friends. combines the lively youthfulness of "The Breakfast Club" and the philosophical dialogue of a Richard Linklater film with a passive yet observant protagonist (think "Midnight in Paris") in order to tell a heartfelt and dreamlike drama about friendship, existentialism and anxiety.
